LATITUDE VS. TEMPERATURE

An analysis was done looking at temperature vs latitude data for 500 plus random cities at varying latitudes to see if there were any discernable trends

CITY LATITUDES VS. MAX TEMPERATURES

Figure 1 shows the relationship between latitude and temperature. We see a very discernable trend. Temperatures are highest closest to the equator and reduces as you move towards the poles. For a closer look at this plot, click here

We took a deeper dive into the data by looking at the data in each hemisphere. A smiliarity in the data in the Northern and Southern hemisphere suggests that the trend is less likely to be random and more likely to be related to latitude.
