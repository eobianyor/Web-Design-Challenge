LATITUDE VS. CLOUDINESS

An analysis was done looking at cloudiness vs latitude data for 500 plus random cities at varying latitudes to see if there were any discernable trends

CITY LATITUDES VS. CLOUDINESS

Figure 1 shows the relationship between latitude and cloudiness. We don't see any discernable trend in this dataset. Cloudiness values are fairly random and show no patterns either close to the equator or close to the poles. For a closer look at this plot, click here

We took a deeper dive into the data by looking at the data in each hemisphere. A smiliarity in the data in the Northern and Southern hemisphere suggests that the trend is less likely to be random and more likely to be related to latitude.
