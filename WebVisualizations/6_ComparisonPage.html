COMPARISONS

Our analysis shows some weather attributes like temperature show a strong correlation to latitude while others like windspeed show a somewhat weaker correlation and others yet like humidity and cloudiness show no correlation at all to the latitude of the city.

STRONG CORRELATION

Max temperature was the weather attribute that showed the strongest correlation with latitude. This was somewhat expected. We have correlation values greater than 0.6 and less than -0.7.

WEAK CORRELATION

Weather attributes like wind speed showed a correlation - albeit a weak one - with latitude. This correlation was consistent and was observed in the Northern and Southern hemisphere datasets. Correlation values greater than 0.1 and less than -0.3.

NO CORRELATION

Weather attributes like cloudiness and humidity showed no discernable correlation with latitude in our dataset. We have correlation values less than 0.2.
